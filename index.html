Speaking of dreams...

"You are never too old to set another goal or to dream a new dream." - Les Brown

A firm believer in Brown's sentiment and the notion of never-ending learning, I embarked on a new journey to challenge myself. After a decade of incredible experiences on over 25 projects as Assistant Editor and Editor, I decided to change course.

My curious nature led me to discover the world of design thinking and interactive experiences in the form of the Bachelor of Design at the University of Sydney.

From delving into Gestalt theory to programming fundamentals, I enjoy growing my skillset to adapt to today's fast-paced digital landscape. This new chapter has broadened my horizons, opening up a world of new opportunities.

At Deloitte Digital, I gained insights into solving customer challenges through design strategy and user research. Currently at Adobe, I am deepening my understanding of personalised experiences and the dynamics of a big tech business.
