Speaking of dreams...

"You are never too old to set another goal or to dream a new dream." - Les Brown

A firm believer in Brown's sentiment and the notion of lifelong learning, I embarked on a new journey to challenge myself. After a decade of working on over 25 projects as Assistant Editor and Editor, I chose to change course.

My curious nature led me to explore the world of interactive experiences and human-centred design (HCD) through Bachelor of Design (formerly Bachelor of Design Computing) at the University of Sydney.

From Gestalt theory to programming fundamentals, I embraced the opportunity to expand my skillset for today's digital landscape. This new chapter has broadened my horizons and opened new doors.

As a Deloitte Digital vacationer, I gained insights into solving customer challenges through strategy and user research. During my six-month Adobe internship, I gained hands-on experience in solutions consulting and bid management, while deepening my understanding of big tech dynamics.

Feeling joyful on my first day at The University of Sydney!
